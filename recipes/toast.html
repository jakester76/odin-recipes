Toast

Description

This is easy. You need not worry. Follow along and you'll be eating toast before you know it.

Necessary supplies are simple. Toasters are most conventional, but other heat sources could be substituted.

Ingredients

  • Slice(s) of bread

Steps

  1. Confirm you have ingredients and supplies listed above.
  2. Stick the bread slice(s) into the toaster.
  3. Start the toaster, following the instructions found in the manual. The longer you set the toast, the more cooked and darker/more burned it'll be. This is your preference.
  4. Enjoy, with butter, jam, or jelly, or nothing else at all.
